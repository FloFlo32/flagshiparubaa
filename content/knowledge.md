# Flagship Aruba knowledge base

Source: https://www.flagshiparuba.com (this is an unofficial redesign concept, not the
live business site).

## About
Flagship Aruba began as a shared dream between three lifelong friends with deep roots in
the maritime world. After years working on the water, they turned passion, experience,
and friendship into a small but unforgettable cruise company in Aruba. As kids they
always joked about owning a pirate ship one day, today that childhood dream sails
Aruba's turquoise waters as a beautifully crafted wooden schooner. All cruises depart
from the pier in front of MooMba Beach Bar on Palm Beach.

## Tours
1. **Morning Splash Adventure** — $69 per guest, 4 hours, departs 9:00 AM. Start the day
   with a snorkel cruise through crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life. Includes
   gear, drinks, and sunny vibes, perfect for early adventurers. Children under 10 welcome
   at a reduced rate.
2. **Afternoon Pirate Party** — $79 per guest, 3 hours, departs 1:00 PM. A lively midday
   tour with music, rope swinging at the SS Antilla, premium snorkel spots, and an open
   bar. Great for thrill-seekers who love fun, sun, and a bit of pirate mischief.
3. **Sunset & Stars Cruise** — $89 per guest, 2.5 hours, departs at sunset (time varies by
   season). Unwind as the sun dips below the horizon on this magical evening sail. Local
   bites, drinks, and ocean breezes, perfect for couples or friends looking to chill.
   Children under 10 welcome at a reduced rate.

Every cruise carries up to 40 guests and includes a self-service open bar (cocktails,
local beers, wine, soft drinks, juices). Groups and private charters (birthdays,
bachelorettes, corporate outings) are welcome, book via the "Book for Groups" or
"Book for Private Events" section on the homepage, or contact us directly.

## Snorkel sites
- **Boca Catalina Reefs** — calm, shallow reef close to shore, great visibility.
- **Malmok Beach** — one of Aruba's top snorkel sites, near the island's north tip.
- **SS Antilla** — Aruba's most famous snorkel site, a WWII-era shipwreck resting just
  below the surface.

## FAQ
- Are Flagship Aruba tours suitable for children? On the Morning Splash Adventure and
  Sunset & Stars Cruise, children under 10 are welcome at a reduced rate.
- What's included in the price? Snorkel gear, flotation vests, and a self-service open
  bar on every cruise; snacks or local bites on the Afternoon Pirate Party and Sunset &
  Stars Cruise.
- Where do cruises depart from? The pier in front of MooMba Beach Bar, Palm Beach.
  Arrive 15 minutes before departure.
- What should I bring? Swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and a camera. Gear and drinks are
  provided.
- Which snorkel sites will we visit? Boca Catalina Reefs, Malmok Beach, and the SS
  Antilla shipwreck, depending on the cruise.
- Can I book a large group or private event? Yes, groups and private charters are
  welcome via the homepage's "Book for Groups" / "Book for Private Events" section, or by
  contacting us directly.
- What happens if the weather is bad? We reschedule at no extra charge if conditions are
  unsafe to sail.
- Do I need snorkeling experience? No, the crew briefs everyone first and all sites suit
  beginners.
- Is gratuity included? No, it's appreciated but never required.
- How do I book? Tap any "Book Now" button to open the secure booking widget.
