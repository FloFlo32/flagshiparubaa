## About
Flagship Aruba began as a shared dream between three lifelong friends with deep roots in
the maritime world. After years working on the water, they turned passion, experience,
and friendship into a small but unforgettable cruise company in Aruba. As kids they
always joked about owning a pirate ship one day, today that childhood dream sails
Aruba's turquoise waters as a beautifully crafted wooden schooner. All cruises depart
from the pier in front of MooMba Beach Bar on Palm Beach.

## Tours
1. **Morning Splash Adventure** — Start the day with a snorkel cruise through
   crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life. Includes gear, drinks, and sunny vibes,
   perfect for early adventurers.
2. **Afternoon Pirate Party** — A lively midday tour with music, rope swinging, premium
   snorkel spots, and an open bar. Great for thrill-seekers who love fun, sun, and a bit
   of pirate mischief.
3. **Sunset & Stars Cruise** — Unwind as the sun dips below the horizon on this magical
   evening sail. Local bites, drinks, and ocean breezes, perfect for couples or friends
   looking to chill.

The signature cruise is a two-hour journey across the crystal-clear Caribbean starting
around $69 per guest, including a sharing platter and a self-service open bar (cocktails,
local beers, wine, soft drinks, juices).

## Snorkel sites
- **Boca Catalina Reefs** — calm, shallow reef close to shore, great visibility.
- **Malmok Beach** — one of Aruba's top snorkel sites, near the island's north tip.
- **SS Antilla** — Aruba's most famous snorkel site, a WWII-era shipwreck resting just
  below the surface.

## FAQ
- Are Flagship Aruba tours suitable for children? On the Sail, Snorkel, Swim & Swing
  Cruise, Afternoon Snorkel, and Sunset Sail Cruises, children under 10 are very welcome
  and can join at a reduced rate.
- Are the Grub & Grog Cruises suitable for children? No, those cruises are adults-only.
